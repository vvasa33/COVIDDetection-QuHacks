FAQs



Where can you be tested for Covid-19?

COVID-19 tests are available at no cost nationwide at health centers and select pharmacies, but if you have symptoms of COVID-19 and want to get tested, call your healthcare provider first.

Is there a vaccine for covid-19?

Yes. However, it is still being tested. It will take a while before it reaches doctors.

What should I put on before I go somewhere?

One of the best practices to make is to wear a mask every time you leave your house. It is also recommended to wear gloves whenever you go to a public area.

What is this website made for?

This website is made to help people have an understanding of how widespread the COVID-19 virus is in their state.

I have a question about the COVID-19 pandemic. Where can I ask it?

You can google up your question to get accurate results from trusted professionals. Government webpages like cdc.gov are very useful, so go check them out!

I have a question about the website. Where can I ask it?

If you have any questions, you can ask about it to one of the developers of the page. Keep reading the FAQ to learn more.
